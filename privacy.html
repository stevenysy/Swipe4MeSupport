Privacy Policy

Swipe4Me collects limited personal information in order to provide core app functionality.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a unique user ID through Firebase Authentication.
  • User Content: If you choose to upload a profile picture, that image is stored and associated with your account.
  • Diagnostics: We may collect basic diagnostic information to maintain app reliability and performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To display profile information within the app
  • To maintain and improve app functionality

Data Storage and Third Parties

Account information and user content are processed and stored using Firebase, a service provided by Google. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. Upon account deletion, associated personal data and profile images are removed from our systems within a reasonable period.
